Lake life, the way it's meant to be

Miller Landing Lakeside RV Park puts you right where you want to be: on the shore of Lake Eufaula, Oklahoma's largest lake. Follow our walking trail straight to a sandy beach made for swimming, sunbathing, and casting a line from the bank.

We're just around the corner from the Duchess Creek Boat Ramp and only minutes from Duchess Creek Marina, lakeside cafes, and local shops. Whether you're here for a weekend getaway or settling in full time, you'll have everything you need and a front-row seat to the water.

Park Rules & Policies

General Rules

  • Speed limit 10 MPH throughout the park
  • Guests must leave park by 11:00 AM (checkout)
  • One self-contained RV per campsite, no tents allowed
  • Do not change sites without prior approval from office
  • Make sure your sewer hose has a tight rubber collar
  • Do not park on or place rugs on grass areas
  • No washing cars or RVs on property
  • No open fires, grilling is okay
  • Do not dispose of fish waste in the dumpster
  • No storage items under your RV
  • No "For Sale" items on display
  • No business or commercial activity permitted

Quiet Hours & Conduct

  • Quiet time: 11:00 PM to 8:00 AM daily
  • Music and conversations must not be heard beyond your site
  • Campers disturbing neighbors after quiet hours will be asked to leave
  • Quiet motorcycles allowed
  • Disorderly conduct, offensive language, and firearms will not be tolerated
  • Alcoholic beverages permitted in moderation at your site only
  • No alcohol consumption by minors
  • Keep your site clean, neat, and orderly at all times

Pet Etiquette

  • Pets must be on a leash no longer than 6 feet at all times
  • Never leave pets unattended inside or outside your RV
  • Pets are not allowed inside or around buildings or play areas
  • Pet owners must pick up after their pets immediately, anywhere on the property
  • Barking dogs will not be tolerated
  • Violation of any rule may result in immediate eviction without a refund

Payment & Cancellation

  • A valid card on file is required for all reservations
  • Daily and weekly stays are due at check-in
  • Monthly rent is due on the 1st; late fees apply after the grace period
  • Electricity for monthly and long-term stays is billed at 16¢/kWh
  • A relocation fee may apply if you request a site change
  • Cancellations: please call us at (918) 680-1588 as soon as plans change and we will walk you through the policy for your stay

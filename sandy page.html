Welcome to Miller Landing

Lake Life the Way It's Meant to Be

Miller Landing Lakeside RV Park puts you right where you want to be — on the shores of Lake Eufaula, Oklahoma's largest lake. Follow our walking trail to a gorgeous sandy beach for swimming, sunbathing, or casting a line from the bank.

We're located just around the corner from the Duchess Creek Boat Ramp and only minutes from Duchess Creek Marina, lakeside cafes, and local shops. Whether you're here for a long weekend or calling this home full-time, Miller Landing is your perfect base camp.

Things To Do

Lake Eufaula Is Your
Playground

600 miles of shoreline and endless outdoor adventure — there's never a dull moment at Oklahoma's Crown Jewel.

🎣 Fishing

World-Class Bass & Catfish

Lake Eufaula is legendary for fishing — catfish, crappie, largemouth bass, and sand bass fill these waters. Fish right from our beach trail or launch at nearby Duchess Creek Ramp.

⛵ Boating

Boating & Water Sports

143,700 acres of sparkling water awaits. The Duchess Creek Boat Ramp is right around the corner — get on the water fast and explore Lake Eufaula's hidden coves and open waters.

🥾 Outdoors

Hiking, Wildlife & Golf

Explore nature trails where deer and wild turkey roam. Two 18-hole golf courses nearby at Lake Eufaula State Park. Plus 31,800 acres of public hunting land surrounding the lake.

Good to Know

Park Rules & Policies

🚗 General Rules

  • Speed limit 10 MPH throughout the park
  • Guests must leave park by 11:00 AM (checkout)
  • One self-contained RV per campsite — no tents allowed
  • Do not change sites without prior approval from office
  • Make sure your sewer hose has a tight rubber collar
  • Do not park on or place rugs on grass areas
  • No washing cars or RVs on property
  • No open fires — grilling is okay
  • Do not dispose of fish waste in the dumpster
  • No storage items under your RV
  • No "For Sale" items on display
  • No business or commercial activity permitted

🌙 Quiet Hours & Conduct

  • Quiet time: 11:00 PM to 8:00 AM daily
  • Music and conversations must not be heard beyond your site
  • Campers disturbing neighbors after quiet hours will be asked to leave
  • Quiet motorcycles allowed
  • Disorderly conduct, offensive language, and firearms will not be tolerated
  • Alcoholic beverages permitted in moderation at your site only
  • No alcohol consumption by minors — will not be tolerated
  • Keep your site clean, neat, and orderly at all times

🐾 Pet Etiquette

  • Pets must be on a leash no longer than 6 feet at all times
  • Never leave pets unattended inside or outside your RV
  • Pets are NOT allowed inside or around buildings, playground, or pool areas
  • Pet owners MUST pick up after their pets immediately, anywhere on the property
  • Barking dogs will not be tolerated
  • Violation of any rule may result in immediate eviction without a refund

💳 Payment & Fees

  • Valid card on file required with automatic recurring payments
  • 1st month fees + $100 electrical deposit due at reservation
  • Rates based on minimum 30-night reservation for monthly stays
  • $50.00 late fee applied 1st day after due date
  • Additional $2/day starting 5th day of month if unpaid
  • $250 site relocation fee if a site change is requested
  • Electricity metered at 16¢ per kWh — read on rent due date

📋 Cancellation Policy

More than 30 days before arrival: Full refund issued, less a transaction fee of 10% of the booking fee or a minimum of $10.00.

14–30 days before arrival: 50% of the payment is forfeited.

Less than 14 days before arrival: The full amount of the reservation payment is forfeited.

Monthly or Longer Stays: Cancellations may be made for a full refund of your deposit up to 60 days prior to your scheduled check-in date. Cancellations made after that 60-day mark but prior to arrival will forfeit all required deposits previously paid. No refunds will be given for any cancellation following your check-in date. All refunds will be made in the same form of payment used to secure the reservation.

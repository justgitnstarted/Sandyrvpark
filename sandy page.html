Welcome to Paradise

Where the Sand Meets the Open Road

Tucked along the shimmering shores of Lake Eufaula — Oklahoma's largest lake with over 600 miles of gorgeous shoreline — Sandy's RV Park Paradise is the lakeside escape you've been dreaming of. With just 12 intimate sites, we offer what big RV parks can't: peace, privacy, and a genuine connection to nature.

Step off your rig and dig your toes into the soft sandy beach. Watch pelicans glide over the water at sunrise. Cast a line from the bank at dusk. This is your home away from home — and it's absolutely beautiful.

Things To Do

Lake Eufaula Is Your
Playground

With 600 miles of shoreline and endless outdoor adventure, there's never a dull moment at Oklahoma's Crown Jewel.

🎣 Fishing

World-Class Bass & Catfish

Lake Eufaula is legendary for fishing — catfish, crappie, largemouth bass, and sand bass thrive in these waters. Two record-holding fish have been pulled from this lake. Your record could be next.

⛵ Boating

Boating & Water Sports

143,700 acres of sparkling water awaits. Launch your boat from nearby ramps, explore hidden coves, jet ski, wakeboard, or simply drift and enjoy the scenery.

🥾 Outdoors

Hiking, Wildlife & Golf

Explore miles of trails where deer, wild turkey, and waterfowl roam freely. Two 18-hole golf courses nearby, plus 31,800 acres of public hunting land surrounding the lake.
